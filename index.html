Why Japan? The Perfect Case Study

High-Context Communication Culture

What is High-Context Communication?

  • Meaning derived from context, not just words
  • Reliance on nonverbal cues, silence, and shared understanding
  • "Reading the air" (kuuki wo yomu)

Honne vs. Tatemae

  • Honne = true feelings (private)
  • Tatemae = public face (social harmony)
